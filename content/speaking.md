                        <p>Richard Rumelt is a keynote speaker at corporate, government, and non-profit events. An engineer, a professor of strategy, an experienced consultant, and a polished speaker, he has been recognized as one of the world's top strategy gurus by <strong>The Economist</strong>, <strong>The Financial Times</strong>, and Thinkers50.</p>

He shows audiences how to identify the hidden structural hurdles that stall growth and how to build a bridge of coherent action to overcome them. Attendees leave with a heightened understanding of how to turn complex challenges into decisive competitive advantages.</p>
