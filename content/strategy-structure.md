                <div class="prose">
                    <p>Richard Rumelt's doctoral dissertation, published by Harvard Business School Press, became one of the most influential works in strategic management. The book introduced the concept of corporate strategy as a pattern of diversification, demonstrating empirically that strategy matters more than industry structure in explaining firm performance. It laid the groundwork for decades of research on diversification, relatedness, and corporate coherence.</p>
