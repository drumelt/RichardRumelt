                    <div class="book-subtitle">How Leaders Become Strategists</div>
                    <p class="key-messages-heading">Some key messages of this book:</p>
                    <ul class="key-messages">
                        <li>Strategy is a form of problem solving and you cannot solve a problem that you do not comprehend.</li>
                        <li>The most effective leaders become strategists by focusing on the way forward promising the greatest achievable progress—the path whose crux was judged to be solvable.</li>
                        <li>You cannot deduce a good strategy from theory. Much of design is a combination of imagination and knowing about many other designs, copying some elements of each.</li>
                        <li>To be a strategist you will have to keep your actions and policies coherent with each other, not nullifying your efforts by having too many different initiatives or conflicting purposes.</li>
                    </ul>
