About My Talks

Richard Rumelt is a keynote speaker at corporate, government, and non-profit events. An engineer, a professor of strategy, an experienced consultant, and a polished speaker, he has been recognized as one of the world's top strategy gurus by The Economist, The Financial Times, and Thinkers50.

His presentations are built around the same diagnostic frameworks he uses to advise heads of state and CEOs. He shows audiences how to identify the hidden structural hurdles that stall growth and how to build a bridge of coherent action to overcome them. Attendees leave with a heightened understanding of how to turn complex challenges into decisive competitive advantages.
